# HAM radio

### Bands

- VHF: 2-meter or 144-148 MHz
- UHF: 70-centimeter or 440 MHz. The ITU amateur radio allocation is from 430 to 440 MHz; however, some countries, such as the United States, allocate hams 420 to 450 MHz
